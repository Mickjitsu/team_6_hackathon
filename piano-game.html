How To Play

In Game Mode, you can choose Easy Mode to play "Twinkle Twinkle Little Star" or Hard Mode for "Fur Elise."
As you play, you'll watch the notes drop at the correct timing, and your goal is to press the right key as each note reaches the bottom. For every correct key press, you earn 1 point, but if you press the wrong key, you'll lose 1 point. Try to keep your accuracy high and watch your score increase as you improve!
